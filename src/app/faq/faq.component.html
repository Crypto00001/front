Frequently Asked Questions

(FAQs)

  • How can I invest with Kamson Fund?
    To make a investment you must first register. Then you must login to your account using your username and password. All deposits must be made through the Make Deposit section in your account.
  • I wish to invest with Kamson Fund but I don't have an any ecurrency account. What should I do?
    You will require perfect money or bitcoin account to start investing with use.You can open perfect money account for free http://perfectmoney.is You can open a free Bitcoin account here: blockchain.info
  • How can I withdraw my funds?
    Login to your account using your username and password and check the Withdraw section.
  • How long does it take for my deposit to be added to my account?
    Your account will be updated during the 1 calendar day.
  • Can I make an additional deposit to Kamson Fund account once it has been opened?
    Yes, you can. New deposit will be added to your current balance. But you can withdraw any money within 3 calendar days.
  • Do you allow compounding?
    We do not allow compouding, but your deposit will be increased after each withdrawal if you will not withdraw all profit. The remaining part of the profit will be added to the current deposit.
  • Does a daily profit paid directly to my currency account?
    No, profits are gathered on your account.
  • How do you calculate the interest on my account?
    Depending on each plan. Interest on your account is acquired daily and credited to your available balance at the end of each calendar day.
  • After I make a withdrawal request, when will the funds be available on my ecurrency account?
    Withdrawal will be made within 1-2 calendar days manually. Funds are usually available within 24-48 hours. If you don't receive your withdrawn funds please contact us and we will solve your problem promtly.
  • Is it possible to change my investment plan?
    Yes when your balance has reached minimum principal of next plan your investment plan will change after checking within 1 calendar day.
  • Can I make a deposit from Bitcoin account is different from a specified during the registration
    No, we will not accept payments from other Bitcoin accounts.
  • What is referral link?
    It's a link which you can share with your friend and get compensation if he decides to invest. You can find your referral link in Account area in Your Referrals section.
  • How can I change my password and other information?
    You can change your password directly from your Account area by editing it in Account section. Your full name, your country and phone number you can change in Edit Account section.
  • May I open several accounts in your program?
    No. it is strictly forbidden. If we find that one member has more that one account, the account and entire funds will be frozen.
  • What is your system time?
    Our system time is GMT+0 (Greenwich).
  • Can I lose money?
    It is known there is a risk involved with investing in all high yield investment programs. However, there are a few simple ways that can help you to reduce the risk of losing more than you can afford to. First, align your investments with your financial goals, in other words, keep the money you may need for the short-term out of more aggressive investments, reserving those investment funds for the money you intend to raise over the long-term. It's very important for you to know that we are real traders and that we invest members' funds on major investments.

We focus on companies with proven business models that are beyond the early phase stage, and therefore do not engage in venture investments.

We evaluate the long-term potential of an investment solution based on a thorough needs and market trend analysis.
