About us

We have extensive experience and insight into businesses and capital markets

We develop and manage promising opportunities

Pandora Group is an online investment platform that offers private and institutional investors direct and professional access to global investments. Having successfully invested in more than 100 companies, Pandora Group is one of the most active venture capital investors in United Kingdom.

Our select private investments have a strong rationale and attractive deal structure – with a preference for off-market opportunities which can offer superior long-term value.

We are here to make life easier – by providing consolidation of assets across banks, jurisdictions, managers and mandates. Having a picture of the overall wealth allows us to manage asset allocation and risk, to provide solutions, and assist in wealth planning and next-generation issues.
